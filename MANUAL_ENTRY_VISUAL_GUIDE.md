## 🎨 **COLORS & STYLING**

### **Color Palette**

```
🌙 Night Sky: Deep Slate (#1e293b)
🌸 Primary Rose: #fb7185 (bright pink)
💗 Secondary Pink: #f472b6 (medium pink)
🟣 Accent Purple: #d8b4fe (light purple)
💚 Success Green: #10b981 (emerald)
⭐ Stars: #fbbf24 (golden yellow)
🌙 Moon: #fef3c7 (pale yellow)

Text: White or Light Gray on dark background
Buttons: Gradient from rose to pink
Success: Green confirmation
Error: Red warning
```

### **Animations**

```
⭐ Stars: Twinkling (flicker effect, variable speed)
☄️ Shooting Stars: 
   - Spawn rate: ~0.2% per frame
   - Trail: 30 frames with opacity fade
   - Speed: Diagonal across screen
   
🌙 Moon:
   - Glow intensity: Oscillates with sin(time)
   - Radius: 80px moon + 120px glow
   - Bloom effect: Radial gradient
   
🎨 Buttons:
   - Hover: Slight scale + shadow increase
   - Click: Feedback animation
   - Disabled: Grayed out, no hover
   
💫 Transitions:
   - Page load: Fade in
   - Form submit: Smooth transitions
   - Success card: Slide in from top
   - Form reset: Quick fade out
```
